I am a PhD student at the Climate Change Research Centre (CCRC) in Sydney. My background in Earth Sciences and weather risk insurance made me appreciate the importance of understanding climate variability on multiple time scales to improve predictions. I use data analysis and model experiments to understand the dynamics of the tropical climate in relation to the El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO), Indian Ocean surface temperature anomalies, and the inter-basin interactions. I am also interested in data visualisation and illustration for effective science communication.
